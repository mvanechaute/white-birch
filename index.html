Location

White Birch Resort is located 4 miles west of Blackduck, MN and 27 miles north of Bemidji, MN on the north shore of Blackduck Lake. Please Note: MapQuest, Yahoo Maps, and GPS devices sometimes place us in the wrong place since our street was renamed a couple years ago. We suggest using them to gain directions to Blackduck Minnesota 56630, but then use our written directions to get to the resort from there.
